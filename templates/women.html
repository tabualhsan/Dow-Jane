Our Mission

The Dow Jane is inspired to educate individuals about the stock market for those who do not know about the stock market. As the creator of this site, I wanted to target a women audience. Growing up as a woman I was always told to save my money incase I needed it one day and never to invest it into the stock market because it is such a ‘risky move for a woman to invest her money in stocks. So many questions started to float around my mind; “Is it because we get paid lower?”, “Do women do not take financial risks?”, “Do I have to have certification to do it?” All these questions started to arise I started hearing noises around the office and personal life about “What stocks have invested in?”. Then it clicked, it is just the exposure, self-taught education is such a powerful tool so why not educate me on this “secret space”. As I did my research, I found out that women currently only hold 6% of CEO positions at S&P 500 companies. This is alarming as well, so why not create an educational platform that highlights aspects of a company to look into prior to investing in them, I have also highlighted which companies hold a women CEO to also raise awareness that the numbers are low and more women need to hold these positions and inspire more women to invest in each other.
